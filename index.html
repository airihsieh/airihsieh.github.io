About

Background

I hold a B.Eng. in Soil and Water Conservation from National Chung Hsing University in Taiwan. During my studies, I worked on projects involving hydrological modelling, flood vulnerability mapping, and spatial analysis using GIS, which sparked my interest in using data to understand and solve real-world problems.

Since then, I've expanded my analytical skills through hands-on projects using SQL, Python, BigQuery, and Tableau. I enjoy working through the entire analytical process, from cleaning and exploring data to building dashboards that communicate insights clearly and effectively.

Outside of data, I enjoy drawing and playing music. I like bringing the same creativity into my analytical work through thoughtful visualisation and intuitive design. I'm naturally curious and always enjoy learning new tools, technologies, and ways to solve problems.
